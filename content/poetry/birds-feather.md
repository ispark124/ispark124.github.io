---
title: birds of a feather
---
The early birds had long since retired for the Night
They had worms to catch and I had
Sleep to lose

And I found myself
At the front of the train
Where conductors and lost boys
Often lose their sleep

The conductors talked about the new
Engineer--only nineteen--those
Early birds. He just bought himself a McLaren
And he's still living at home

They talked about jealousy
And their wives and the
Cars they would have flown them in.
But for now they drive trains and
Their wives crazy

They said their wives names like the keys
Of their front doors. Clear and
Belonging. Like the more I listened
The closer we got to Home
