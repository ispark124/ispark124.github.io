title: forgotten wishes

And I remember watching her  Well, watching her
Watch the candles  Wax dripping onto her cake her
Eyes like the day

"It's the most beautiful thing 
I've ever seen"

She used to be so angry angry at herself
Like if she was angry enough it'd make the 
Candles stop weeping

Now she doesn't remember  white  hot  knuckles or the
Secrets she kept in the
Bags of her eyes
She doesn't remember 
The yellow blouse we dressed her in today or
Her cotton candy wishes last year or
Yesterday

But she remembers the candles 
And that she's never been
Happier than she is right now

It's the most beautiful thing 
I've ever seen
